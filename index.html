WHY FIS-UEL?

The Information Systems Department at the University of Economics and Law (UEL) is responsible for teaching and research in the field of management information systems and the application of information technology in business. The department focuses on developing human resources who can integrate technology, data, and business knowledge to meet the growing demand for digital transformation in modern organizations.

SCP COURSES: THREE TRACKS

ASIAN INSIGHTS

Asia is a dynamic region that is home to culturally diverse societies at varying stages of transformation. As a cosmopolitan city state with a thriving entrepreneurship and innovation scene, Ho Chi Minh City offers myriad opportunities for all.

DIGITAL INTELLIGENCE

Digital intelligence enables individuals and organisations to adeptly navigate the digital landscape, empowering them to harness technology effectively for success, especially in today's interconnected world.

SUSTAINABLE FUTURES

Sustainability is multifaceted and applicable to social and economic domains such as environmental conservation and business practices. As one of the world's top financial hubs and greenest cities, Ho Chi Minh City is an ideal platform to explore this topic.

COURSES

Gain new insights and perspectives on Asia, Digital Intelligence and Sustainability from UEL faculty.

You may enroll in a maximum of two courses in the same session (session 1 or 2), with the first from the morning courses and the second from the afternoon courses, to avoid a timetable clash.

You can mix and match courses from different tracks.

Each course confers one UEL Credit Unit, equivalent to 6 - 7.5 ECTS, 10 - 12 CATS, 3 - 4 US credits.

Please consult your university administrators on the transfer of the course credits before sending in your application.

(Please note that the course list is subject to change.)
